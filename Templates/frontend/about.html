International Institute of Healthcare Leadership and Quality Improvement (IIHLM) The International Institute of Healthcare Leadership and Quality Improvement (IIHLM) is a leading global organization dedicated to empowering healthcare leaders and professionals to drive positive change within their organizations and healthcare systems.

Our Mission

  1. Africa Reforms
    To catalyse healthcare reforms that will drive development in Africa and targeted at achieving health for all by 2050.

  2. Training
    To train 100, 000 healthcare professionals across the globe by 2030 and 10 000 per year subsequently.

  3. Facilitation
    To put our learning and assessment tools in all tertiary, secondary and primary care hospitals across Africa by 2030. .

  4. Publication
    To facilitate research publications and translation of evidence based global best practices by way of adoption and adaptation to socioeconomic realities for driving quality care.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to establish ourselves as a global leader in healthcare leadership and quality improvement, striving to rank among the top 10 professional institutes worldwide. We envision a future where our organization plays a pivotal role in shaping the healthcare landscape, driving innovation, and fostering excellence in healthcare delivery. Through our commitment to continuous improvement and advancement, we aim to set new standards of excellence in healthcare leadership, education, and research. Our vision encompasses a world where every individual has access to high-quality healthcare services, regardless of geographical location or socioeconomic status. We aspire to be recognized internationally for our contributions to healthcare reform, innovation, and the pursuit of health equity, ultimately making a meaningful impact on the health and well-being of communities around the globe.
